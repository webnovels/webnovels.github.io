She avoided Jasper's hand, smiling: "Never mind, the 'boss lady' is here, how could I dare to have lunch with the boss alone?"

She winked at Jasper and walked out of the office in her high heels.

"Aren't you thrilled? Finally able to be with her day and night." I couldn't help but jab at him.
