Waking up the next day, it was bright outside. I felt the cold spot beside me. Slowly, I finished breakfast. Jasper was already waiting in the car.
We were going to the family estate today. In the car, we said nothing. Finally, I said, "Sorry about last night." Jasper was working. He glanced at me, "What were you looking for last night?" I paused, then honestly replied, "A love letter."
"You wrote it?" "Yes." Jasper's gaze landed on my face. He asked, "A secret crush, huh?" My cheeks warmed, "It was my senior from high school, very handsome, you know."
I spoke while looking at him. Jasper chuckled briefly, as if unconcerned.
