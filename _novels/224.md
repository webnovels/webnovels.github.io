I asked Declan for a divorce.  
I don't want anything except the money.  
Declan sneered, "Not even your beloved son?"  
"No, not even him."  
Because my child's wish for his fifth birthday was,  
"Can dad and mom get a divorce? I want Aunt Fiona to be my mom."
