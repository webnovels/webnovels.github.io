My wife went on a 3-year business trip abroad. When she returned, I went to pick her up at the airport.

To my shock, she wasn't alone - there was also a 6-month-old baby.

My coworkers joked: "The kid may not be yours, but at least your wife still is!"

But then my wife handed me divorce papers. This time, I was the one who didn't want her anymore.
