As soon as I finished eating and parted ways with Zack, I received a message from my parents.

The Cloud family was very satisfied with me and hoped to proceed with the marriage soon.

I had miscalculated. I had only intended to find some fool to cooperate with me so my parents would stop forcing me to go on blind dates. Who knew I'd end up meeting Zack and digging myself into a big hole.
