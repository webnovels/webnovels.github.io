Staring at my mom's harsh face, I couldn't help but let out a cold laugh, pointing right at her nose.

"When you cheated on Dad and he caught you, making him so angry that he died, did you think about resting peacefully?"

"For years, you've favored your brother, giving everything good to Hazel and her brother, never caring whether I lived or died. And you still have the nerve to call yourself my mom?"

"Since middle school, you haven't given me a penny. I've earned all my tuition and living expenses through my own work. Educate me? You have no right!"
