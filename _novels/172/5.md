Heart...?! I hid back in my room, burying myself in the blankets. But no matter how unwilling, Asher still came.

Before he arrived, his voice preceded him, "Adeline, even this didn't kill you." Asher disdainfully threw a stack of papers at my face, "Sign it." It was an organ donation agreement. Donor: Adeline. Recipient: Beatrix. Organ: Heart.
